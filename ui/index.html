CUSTOM SETUP OPEN DASHBOARD

AUTOPILOT: drop a resume, walk away. The skill infers your profile, builds your evidence vault, searches every job board, scores every match, tailors resumes for your top 5, builds packets for your top 3, and re-runs the search every 24 hours. You review and apply.

01

RESEARCH

Search, score, study. Nothing is sent anywhere. Best for passive browsing or learning a market before you commit.

  • Multi-source search across 11 boards
  • Evidence-backed match scores
  • No submissions, no outreach
02

ASSISTED DEFAULT

Search, score, tailor, build application packets. You click the actual apply button on each site — the tool does everything up to that point.

  • Tailored resume + cover letter per job
  • Provenance for every claim
  • Honesty report flags any gap
03

AUTO TOS RISK

Programmatic submission via official APIs only (Greenhouse / Lever / Ashby). Off by default. Requires explicit opt-in.

  • Allowlist sources only
  • Daily cap + minimum score
  • Single-button kill switch

The Career Evidence Vault

Every claim that appears in a tailored resume is traceable to a verified source — a past resume, a LinkedIn export, a GitHub repo, a portfolio page, an uploaded doc. If the engine can’t tie a sentence to evidence, it doesn’t write the sentence. It flags a gap instead.

Compliance, in plain English

  • We never bypass logins, CAPTCHAs, or rate limits.
  • Scraping sources (LinkedIn / Indeed / Glassdoor) are marked GRAY; we render assisted packets and you submit yourself.
  • Official APIs (Greenhouse / Lever / Ashby / Remotive RSS) are marked LEGAL and may be used for auto-apply once you opt in.
  • You can delete every byte of your data from Settings.
